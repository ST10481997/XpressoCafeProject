Xpresso Café?

Xpresso Café is a Café that was founded by two individuals, Nicole and Tomer Elhadad.It was founded in 2016, Durbanville, with the concept of serving coffee and delicios backed goods to everyone at an affordale price.

Why R14?

Xpresso Café believes that coffee is more than a drink, it is a way of bringing people together. It opened its doors in 2020 and since then we have been working hard to provide high quality coffee, freshly baked pastries and create a warm environment for customers. This goes along with their motto "With one cup at a time," they want to impact peoples lives positively.

Xpresso Café is the first R10 Take-Away shop in South Africa to sell all their item for ONLY R10 each. We managaed to maintain our prices until October 2021, after that prices rose to R12 In October 2024, the price increased again to R14(current price).
Xpresso Café's goal is to provide everyone great, flavourful coffee, delicios fresh pastries, mouthwatering sandwiches and treats to enjoy anywhere and anytime.
We currently have 72 stores in Gauteng, Western Cape and KwaZulu-Natal.
